Dollarydoos

Dollarydoos is a simple money tracker, which I'm still working on in private. Once I get the foundation done, I will publisize it. It will basically be a way to keep track of your dollarydoos, wherever they are. This app will include budgets, funds, and accounts. Budgets will allow you to keep track on what you want to spend in different categories and how much you can still spend in those categories. Funds will keep track of a goal for a purchace, or set of purchaces. To fulfill the goal, you can move money from an account to the fund and will add to the goal's fulfillment. Accounts are containers for your money which keep track of the changes in the ammounts.

Keep an eye out for this project, because I believe that it should see a release or two.
